S(HE)TEM

A resource page to give young women an easy access to tools that would help them suceed in the STEM fields

Inspiration & Purpose:

Started from Kode With Klossy, my friend and I wanted to create something value for something we both cared passionately about.

We built this site to help give access to resources for young women. Some of the resources include interviews from many successful women in STEM, DIY projects, and much more!
