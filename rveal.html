RVEAL

RVEAL is an online platform that allows users to easily discover small female owned businesses, while also empowering them.

OVERVIEW

Rveal is the perfect alternative to shopping at big corporations and suppliers. Often, small businesses specialize in a few items so consumers find it hard to consistently shop at small businesses. However, Rveal allows all female owned small businesses to gather in one spot allowing users to shop at multiple small businesses at once. Through it's female exclusivity, Rveal empowers and supports females.
